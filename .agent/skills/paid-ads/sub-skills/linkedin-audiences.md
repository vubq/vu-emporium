# LinkedIn Audiences

**Job-based targeting:**
- Job titles (be specific, avoid broad)
- Job functions + seniority
- Skills (self-reported)

**Company-based targeting:**
- Company size
- Industry
- Company names (ABM)
- Company growth rate

**Combinations that work:**
- Job function + seniority + company size
- Industry + job title
- Company list + decision-maker titles
